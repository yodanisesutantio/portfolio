KEMUDI

A Web-Based Driving School App

KEMUDI, Yodanis' current development endeavor, is a web app designed to connect students, instructors, and driving school admins. KEMUDI's user-friendly interface guides novice drivers on their journey to mastery, supported by experienced instructors in the challenging landscape of modern driving.
